The `\require{cancel}` might be needed in some Math Renderer to enable cancle symbols.

$$
\begin{align}
\frac{a}{b} & = c \\ \\
b \cdot \frac{a}{b} & = c \cdot b \\
\cancel{b} \cdot \frac{a}{\cancel{b}} & = c \cdot b \\ \\
a & = cb \\ \\
\frac{a}{c} & = \frac{cb}{c} \\ \\
\frac{a}{c} & = \frac{\cancel{c}b}{\cancel{c}} \\ \\
\frac{a}{c} & = b
\end{align}
$$
