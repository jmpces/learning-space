## **Parabola**

### **Equation**

- **Vertical Facing**

    ***standard form*** :
    $(x-h)^2 = 4a(y-k)$

- **Horizontal Facing**

    ***standard form*** :
    $(y-k)^2 = 4a(x-h)$

**Length of the Latus Rectum** = $4a$

**Latus Rectum Coordinate** = $\pm 2a$, add to the focus

### **Coordinates**

- **Vertex/Center** $(h,k)$

- **Focus**
    - horizontal facing : $(h + a, k)$
    - vertical facing : $(h, k + a)$

- **Directrix**
    - horizontal facing : $x = h - a$
    - vertical facing : $y = k - a$
